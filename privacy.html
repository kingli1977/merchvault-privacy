Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

MerchVault is an anime merchandise collection manager that helps you track your figures, badges, standees, and more. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. MerchVault stores all your collection information locally on your device. We do not have access to, nor do we store, any of your data on our servers.

Data Storage

  • All collection data (items, photos, notes) is stored locally on your device using secure, on-device storage.
  • No data is transmitted to any external servers or third-party services.
  • Photos are stored in the app's private document directory on your device.
  • If you use the export feature, data is saved to a location you choose (e.g., iCloud Drive, Files app).

Camera & Photo Library Access

MerchVault requests access to your camera and photo library solely for the purpose of adding photos of your merchandise to your collection. Photos are stored locally within the app and are never uploaded or shared.

In-App Purchases

MerchVault offers a Pro subscription (monthly or yearly) to unlock unlimited collection items and premium features. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking SDKs, or third-party advertising frameworks. We have zero insight into how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

MerchVault does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
