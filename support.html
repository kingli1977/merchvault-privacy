Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new item to my collection?

Tap the + button on the home screen. You can add photos, set the category, price, and notes for each item.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore purchases". Your Pro subscription will be restored automatically.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your collection data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any personal data.

How do I export my collection?

Go to Settings and use the "Export data" option to save your collection as a JSON file.

What's included in the Pro subscription?

Pro unlocks unlimited collection items and premium features like advanced statistics and data import/export.
